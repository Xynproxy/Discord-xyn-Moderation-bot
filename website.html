Moderation Bot

Welcome to the Moderation Bot! This bot helps with managing your server, offering commands like muting, kicking, banning, and more.

Commands

  • !mute [user]: Mute a user.
  • !unmute [user]: Unmute a user.
  • !ban [user]: Ban a user.
  • !unban [user ID]: Unban a user by their ID.
  • !warn [user] [reason]: Warn a user.
  • !unwarn [user]: Remove a user's warn.
  • !kick [user]: Kick a user.
